sunday shades

it is sunday again the church
bells are ringing the lampshade
rocking the light to a pale morning

no i don't chase rainbows of overflowing
glass flooding the edges of my universe

no i don't dream anymore
no i don't sleep anymore

the morning withers the light
breaks at the wall that separates
space from space from space

and i exist
circling in a fishbowl
